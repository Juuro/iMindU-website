iMindU

iMindU is a lightweight helper app for fast adding a reminder to the iPhone calendar.

Get iMindU now! »

Like Reminder.app, but ...

... faster! With iMindU you can set your Reminder in a few seconds. Just pick a title, and in how many minutes / hours / days you would like to be reminded. You select the unit of time.

The power of iCloud

Because of iCloud your Reminders are always available on all your devices. Even on your non-iOS-Devices, like Mac OS X and Windows.
